Being Broody

Spring 2014

The Project

Being Broody is a multiplayer game where players assume the position of a mother bird incubating her eggs. Players don a mask and sit on a nest of handmade eggs with arduinos and thermistors embedded within them. The player that maintains the optimal incuation temperature wins.
