Design should make life better.

Hello! I'm a product designer at IBM working on building a smarter planet. My process centers around listening, understanding, tinkering and prototyping. Nothing makes me happier than working with others to create great digital experiences. I think that good design should make life a little better.

While interning at Amazon and Disney Interactive, I fell in love with the product design process. I graduated from the University of Southern California School of Cinematic Arts' Interactive Media Division in 2015. There, I studied storytelling, interactive design and data visualization. At the USC Mobile and Environmental Media Lab I assisted with several research projects relating to the Internet of Things.

On the weekends, I love to explore the National Park System, take pictures and watch too many movies.
