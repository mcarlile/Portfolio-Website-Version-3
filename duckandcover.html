Duck & Cover

Fall 2014

The Project

Duck & Cover is an interactive classroom wartime simulator that puts players in the shoes of Eddie, a student at Fairfield Elementary School in 1952. Listen to informative and entertaining lessons from Bert the Turtle as you learn how to protect yourself from Nuclear fallout by hiding under a classroom desk. This game works best in a large room that is at least 20 ft. wide, as players will be asked to move their bodies in physical space.
