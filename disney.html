Design Goals

  • Allow guests to easily find great Disney content
  • Create a safe and beautiful place for kids to hang out with Disney characters
  • Make Disney.com a leader in responsive design
  • Allow corporate partners to showcase offers

Design Constraints

  • Limited access to user researchers
  • Any design had to be implemented without significant development resources
  • Quick turn around
  • Must remain COPPA compliant
